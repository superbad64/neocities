Lean mean (optionally) green machine

Find it on GitHub !

I actually don't have a whole lot to say about this case, not that it's a bad thing. It went through a fair amount of revisions - the first few were made to be one-shot chassis printable on my old SOVOL SV06+, which has a 300 by 300mm print area. Later, in an effort to make it more "accessible", I split it into parts that could fit on a more standard 220 by 220mm print bed.

This case supports 24mm screw-in buttons1 as well as low and flat collar joysticks. It's still a fairly cramped build though, while a standard Brook harness will fit you'll have to compress everything a little bit with the bottom plates.

I really don't know what to add to that ! It's a very solid baseline case with a good feature set and buildability. And it's rather inexpensive to boot, I clocked it at roughly 550 grams (depending on infill of course), so barely over half of a spool ! Go print it now !!

- Bad64

1: At this point it should be known that I really hate snap-ins
